Peng Shuai, a well-known Chinese tennis player, suddenly posted a post on Weibo last night, shockingly having an unfavorable relationship with Zhang Gaoli, the former Vice Premier of the State Council of China, and accused Zhang Gaoli’s wife Kang Jie of being like the "Queen of Zhen Huan", not only knowing, but indulging Zhang Gao Li vented her desires and ridiculed Peng Shuai.

It is reported that Zhang Gaoli was born in 1946 and is now 75 years old, while Peng Shuai was born in 1986. The difference between the two is 40 years old. Peng Shuai admitted that the two had had sexual relations and even had feelings 7 years ago, but after Zhang Gaoli went to Beijing to be promoted to the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China, she severed contact with her. But Peng Shuai said that just 3 years ago, Zhang Gaoli contacted her again after retiring and brought her home with his wife Kang Jie. In front of Kang Jie, he asked for sex with Peng Shuai again.

Peng Shuai said that she was unwilling at first, but at this time Zhang Gaoli once again played a sweet-talking offensive, saying to her: "The universe is very big and the earth is a grain of sand in the universe.", "I have never forgotten you in the past 7 years. "Peng Shuai admitted that under Zhang Gaoli’s sweet words, she felt softened, so she had sex with Zhang Gaoli again.

However, Peng Shuai also accused Zhenggong Kangjie that although Zhenggong Kangjie acquiesced in her unfavorable relationship with Zhang Gaoli, but at the same time described Kang Jie as "the Queen of Zhen Huan Chuan", Peng Shuai said: "Your wife is like the Queen of Zhen Huan Chuan, and I can't describe how much I am. It’s unbearable. Many times I think I’m still alone? I think I’m a zombie, pretending, pretending every day, which one is the real me?” Peng Shuai accused that Kang Jie looked the same in front of Zhang Gaoli. The queen is the same again, ridiculing Peng Shuai many times in private.

Peng Shuai said that she had never taken a cent from Zhang Gaoli, and the only thing she wanted was a name, but while Zhang Gaoli impressed her with sweet words, she also said that she could not get a divorce because of her high position. Zhang Gaoli even asked Peng Shuai, Don't tell your family about the improper relationship between them. Peng Shuai said that the two had made an appointment to "talk slowly" on the 2nd of this month, but Zhang Gaoli said on the phone that "contact again if you have something", and then suddenly disappeared. Peng Shuai said: "It's just like 7 years ago. Don't do it anymore." So last night, he directly posted a message on Weibo to break the news.

But netizens also observed that about 20 minutes after Peng Shuai posted a post on Weibo, the entire article was emptied, and even the articles reposted by other netizens were "the boat crossed the water without a trace" and disappeared without a trace. Now, even if I input keywords such as "Peng Shuai" and "Zhang Gaoli" on Weibo, no articles or messages about the two people's unethical relationship can be found at all, and Weibo is completely blocked. (Shi Yijie/Comprehensive Foreign Telegraph Report)
